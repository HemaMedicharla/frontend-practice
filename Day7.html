Egg Omelet Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • chopped onion
  • chopped chilli
  • Salt as needed
  • a pinch of turmeric
  • Oil or Butter
  • Coriander leaves yourwish

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add chopped onion, chilli, salt, and turmeric,coriander leaves
  3. Beat the mixture well.
  4. Heat a pan and add a little oil or butter.
  5. Pour the egg mixture onto the pan.
  6. Cook on medium flame until the bottom is set.
  7. Flip the omelet and cook for another minute.
  8. Serve hot.
